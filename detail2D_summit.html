Our Journey on The Day 1

  • 06:00 AM Wake up, preparation and have a breakfast at Hotel.
  • 07:00 AM Drive to Sembalun Village (1,100 m) and check in at the Rinjani National Park office.
  • 08:00 AM Begin trekking from Sembalun village with a gentle climb through open meadows.
  • 10:00 AM Arrive at Pos 1 "Pemantauan" (1,300 AMSL) have a water and snack pause.
  • 11:00 AM Arrive at Pos 2 "Tengengean" (1,500 AMSL) have a rest and pause for lunch.
  • 13:00 PM Arrive at Pos 3 "Pada Balong" (1,800 AMSL) have a water and snack pause.
  • 14:00 PM After lunch and a short rest, continue the trek to Sembalun Crater Rim (2,639 AMSL), a challenging 3-hour climb with steep sections.
  • 17:00 PM Camp overnight at the Crater Rim (Pelawangan), where you can savor breathtaking views of both the sunrise and sunset.
  • 17:30 PM Dinner will be served as the sun sets above the clouds, offering a truly unforgettable experience. As night falls, the sky will come alive with stars, providing incredible stargazing opportunities.
  • 19:00 PM Have a wonderfull dinner and sleep for recovery.

Journey on Day 2

  • 02:30 AM Wake up, preparation and have a light breakfast.
  • 03:00 AM Begin the hike towards the summit of Mount Rinjani (3,726 m), a journey that takes approximately 3 to 5 hours, depending on your pace.
  • 06:00 AM At the summit, enjoy breathtaking panoramic views of Lombok Island, Bali, Sumbawa, and Segara Anak Lake.
  • 07:00 AM Descend to the Pelawangan Camp for breakfast.
  • 09:30 AM Pack your belongings and make your way back to Sembalun village via the same route.
  • 11:00 PM Arrive at Pos 3 "Pada Balong" (1,800 AMSL) have a water and snack pause.
  • 13:00 PM Arrive at Pos 2 "Tengengean" (1,500 AMSL) have a rest and pause for lunch
  • 14:00 PM Arrive at Pos 1 "Pemantauan" (1,300 AMSL) have a water and snack pause.
  • 15:00 PM Arrive at Starting Point Sembalum Village. Our car will be waiting at the village to transfer you back to Senaru.
  • 16:00 PM Prepare yourself at our office for another transfer to your hotel or to Senggigi, Mataram, or Bangsal harbor if you wish to visit the Gili islands.
  • 17:00 PM End of Journey 2 Days 1 Night - Summit Sembalun Trek Route.
