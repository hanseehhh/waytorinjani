Our Journey on The Day 1

  • 06:30 AM Wake up, preparation and have a breakfast at the Hotel.
  • 07:00 AM Drive to Senaru Gate with guide, potter and our transport to check in at the Rinjani National Park office.
  • 07:30 AM Begin trekking from Senaru Gate with a gentle climb through the rainforest.
  • 09:00 AM Arrive at Pos 1 (915 AMSL) have a water and snack pause.
  • 12:00 PM Arrive at Pos 2 (1,500 AMSL) where the porter prepare lunch lunch for you.
  • 14:00 PM Arrive at Pos 3 "Mondokan Lokak" (2,000 AMSL) have a water and snack pause.
  • 14:30 PM After have a short rest at Pos 3, we proceed to campsite through the open meadows.
  • 16:00 PM Arrive at campsite Senaru Rim (2,641 AMSL) and have a rest.
  • 17:00 PM With tend prepared by our Potters. Enjoy an incredible sunset over Mount Agung in bali and stunning view of Segara Anak Lake and Gunung baru in the edge of the lake.
  • 18:00 PM Have a wonderfull dinner and sleep for recovery.

Journey on Day 2

  • 06:30 AM Wake up at the tend, have breakfast with an amazing view of Segara Anak Lake and creter.
  • 08:00 AM After breakfast and preparation descend back to Senaru Village.
  • 08:30 AM Begin trekking from campsite Senaru Rim decend to Senaru Village. The descent take about 5 hours with lunch along the way.
  • 13:00 PM Arrive at Senaru Village. Where the car will bring you back to the Hotel.
  • 14:00 PM Prepare yourself at our office for your another transfer to your next distinations.
  • 15:00 PM End of Journey 2 Days 1 Night - Senaru Rim Trek Route.
