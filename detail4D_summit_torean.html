Our Journey on The Day 1

  • 06:30 AM Wake up, preparation and have a breakfast at Hotel.
  • 07:00 AM Drive to Sembalun Village (1,100 m) with guide, potter and our transport to check in at the Rinjani National Park office.
  • 08:30 AM Start trekking from Sembalun village with gentle climb through an open meadow.
  • 10:00 AM Arrive at Pos 1 "Pemantauan" (1,300 AMSL) have a water and snack pause.
  • 11:00 AM Arrive at Pos 2 "Tengengean" (1,500 AMSL) have a rest and pause for lunch.
  • 13:00 PM After having lunch at Pos 2, proceed to Pos 3 "Pada Balong".
  • 14:00 PM Arrive at Pos 3 "Pada Balong" (1,800 AMSL) have a water and snack pause.
  • 14:30 PM After a short rest, continue the trek to Sembalun Crater Rim (2,639 AMSL) for rest and having a dinner with beautiful view.
  • 17:00 PM Camp overnight at the Crater Rim (Pelawangan), where you can savor breathtaking views of both the sunrise and sunset.
  • 17:30 PM Dinner will be served as the sun sets above the clouds, offering a truly unforgettable experience. As night falls, the sky will come alive with stars, providing incredible stargazing opportunities.
  • 18:00 PM Have a wonderfull dinner and sleep for recovery.

Journey on Day 2

  • 02:00 AM Wake up, preparation and have a breakfast + hot drink.
  • 02:30 AM Begin the trek to the Peak at 2.30 a.m The trek start moderately, become easier but longer and the final approach involves climbing up step volcanic scree.
  • 06:00 AM Reach the Peak (3,726 AMSL) to witness a beautiful sunrise and panoramic view of lombok island.
  • 07:00 AM Descend to the Sembalun Crater Rim (Pelawangan Campsite) for preparation and have breakfast.
  • 09:00 AM Arrive at Sembalun Crater Rim (Pelawangan Campsite) have a rest and pause for lunch + preparation to leave Pelawangan.
  • 10:00 AM Continue descending to Segara Anak Lake, a 3-hour trek.
  • 13:00 PM Rest by the lake, enjoy the view, swim, and soak in the hot springs known for their natural healing powers. The porters will prepare lunch.
  • 15:00 PM Have a Lunch and enjoy the lake and and soak in the hot spring known for their natural healing power.
  • 16:00 PM Camping by the lake side, and have some rest
  • 18:00 PM Dinner with the sunset view on lake, also can enjoy the hot spring water.
  • 19:00 PM Have a wonderfull sleep for recovery.

Journey on Day 3

  • 06:00 AM Wake up, have a breakfast and Free Time and Rest to restore your energy.
  • 13:00 PM After have lunch, Desceend from the lake side to Kebon jeruk.
  • 14:00 PM Stop by the Gua Susu Hot Spring. Soak your legs for 20 minutes.
  • 14:30 PM Continue descend to Propok 1 hour trek.
  • 15:00 PM stop 20 minutes to enjoy propok panoramic and take some Fotage.
  • 15:30 PM Continue descend to Basecamp Kebon Jeruk 2 hour trek.
  • 17:00 PM Arrive at Kebon Jeruk Campsite, have a rest and dinner.
  • 18:00 PM Have a sleep for recovery.

Journey on Day 4

  • 06:30 AM Wake up, have a breakfast and enjoy breakfast with a stunning panoramic
  • 07:00 AM After having breakfast with a stunning panoramic, we continue for descend to Torean Village through the cliff and rainforest.
  • 08:00 AM We will stop to enjoy the most Beautiful Waterfall and the icon of Torean Treck (Penimbungan Waterfall).
  • 08:30 AM Continue our journey to Torean Village, the descent take about 5-6 with lunch along the way.
  • 14:00 PM Arrive at torean village, where the car will bring you back to our office in Senaru
  • 15:00 PM Prepare yourself at our office for another transfer to your next distination.
  • 16:00 PM End of Journey 4 Days 3 Night - Summit Sembalun-Torean Trek Route.
