Renawadi

Renawadi, the founder of Way to Rinjani, is a native of Senaru, a village nestled at the foot of Mount Rinjani. With a deep-rooted connection to the mountain, he began trekking its trails during his middle school years, gaining invaluable experience navigating its challenging paths. Driven by his passion for Mount Rinjani and his dedication to preserving its natural beauty, Renawadi played a significant role in exploring the Torean route and has actively participated in mountain conservation efforts since childhood.

Way to Rinjani combines Renawadi's local expertise and commitment to sustainability, offering professional and personalized trekking experiences. With a focus on safety, comfort, and environmental stewardship, the company ensures every journey to Mount Rinjani is both memorable and responsible.

Our Potters

At Way to Rinjani, we require all trekking groups to include at least one porter as part of their expedition. This policy not only provides vital support to our guests during the challenging trek but also contributes to the livelihoods of our skilled porters, honoring their expertise and dedication to their profession.

Our porters are highly trained professionals, essential to ensuring a smooth and enjoyable trekking experience. They are adept at carrying gear, preparing meals, and setting up rest areas efficiently and reliably. By choosing Way to Rinjani, you not only enjoy the benefits of their exceptional service but also support their role as indispensable members of the Mount Rinjani trekking community.

Certificate

Way to Rinjani operates with full compliance under formal and verified licenses issued by the national and local government authorities. These authentic permits include the license to establish and run a professional tour company, individual guiding certifications for all our guides, and authorization to manage a basecamp. Additionally, we hold the necessary permits to host international clients for trekking Mount Rinjani.

Our commitment to adhering to legal and professional standards ensures that all aspects of our operations are legitimate and transparent. Guests can trust in the authenticity of our services and our dedication to providing a safe and reliable trekking experience.
