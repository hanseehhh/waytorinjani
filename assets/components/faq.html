Located in Lombok, West Nusa Tenggara, Mount Rinjani is the third highest mountain in Indonesia with an altitude of 3,762MDPL.

The package that is suitable for beginner hikers is a package with 4 days and 3 nights, because with this package we are guaranteed to pass all the destinations even though we are walking very slowly.

The best time to climb Mount Rinjani is during the dry season, which runs from April to December. During this period, the weather is generally clear, making for safer trekking conditions and better visibility. The rainy season (January to March) is not recommended due to slippery trails and limited access.

Yes, a climbing permit is required to climb Mount Rinjani. This permit is included in most tour packages and helps fund the conservation efforts of Mount Rinjani National Park.

We also have a license to set up this business and also a guide license to guide guests to climb Mount Rinjani with procedures that are in accordance with the directives of the Mount Rinjani National Park.
